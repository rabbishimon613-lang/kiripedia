**Mark McDougall** is a U.S. criminal defense attorney and was a member of [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou)'s defense team in the 2012–2013 Espionage Act prosecution under the [Plato Cacheras](/wiki/plato-cacheras)-led firm. Kiriakou identifies him as *"one of the best attorneys I've ever encountered in my life, and the one that I liked and respected the most."*<Cite s="2025-10-10-joe-rogan-experience-2392" t="58:26" />

## The decisive argument

On the morning of the final plea-deal decision — when Cacheras, Bob Trout, and McDougall came to Kiriakou's house to argue him into accepting — McDougall delivered the line Kiriakou cites as the one that broke through:

> You know what your problem is? Your problem is you think this is about justice — and it's not about justice. It's about mitigating damage. Take the deal.<Cite s="2025-10-10-joe-rogan-experience-2392" t="58:26" />

Kiriakou took the deal: thirty months, of which he served twenty-three at [FCI Loretto](/wiki/fci-loretto).<Cite s="2025-10-10-joe-rogan-experience-2392" t="58:58" />
