**Gina Haspel** was the Director of the Central Intelligence Agency under President Trump's first term. Per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) she and Mike Pompeo were the two *"very, very bad people"* whom Trump appointed to the position in his first term.<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="25:51" />

## "Bloody Gina"

Kiriakou: *"The other one was bloody Gina Haspel. We called her bloody Gina for a good reason. She flew out to the secret site to sit in on one of the torture sessions just because she could. Just to sit there and enjoy it and take it all in. What kind of sick person does something like that?"*<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="25:51" /><Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="26:23" />
