**Steve Kappes** is a former Deputy Director of the [Central Intelligence Agency](/wiki/cia). [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) encountered him in the lobby of the Marriott hotel in Sanaa, Yemen, during his final Yemen trip as a Senate Foreign Relations Committee investigator in approximately 2010. The presence of the *"package is moving"* protective-detail call-out alerted Kiriakou to the presence of a senior visitor; Kappes greeted him by name on emerging from the elevator.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:21:55" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:22:57" />
