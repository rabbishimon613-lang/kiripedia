**Trita Parsi** — rendered as "Tita Parsi" in the source-corpus auto-captions — is an Iran scholar, described by [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) as *"one of the greatest Iran scholars on the planet."* On August 10, 2025, Parsi published a piece in *Foreign Affairs* predicting a renewed Israeli–Iranian war by the end of that month — a prediction that was, in Kiriakou's account, *"laid out in very logical fashion."* The prediction is referenced by Kiriakou in support of his broader argument that the United States cannot serve as honest broker in Israeli–Iranian affairs after the conclusion of the [12-Day War](/wiki/iran-12-day-war).<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:48:20" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:48:52" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:49:22" />
