**Eric O'Neill** is a former FBI analyst who, in the final months of the FBI's investigation of senior counterintelligence officer [Robert Hanssen](/wiki/robert-hanssen), was placed as Hanssen's nominal assistant in order to observe him for tradecraft slip-ups that would confirm him as the mole.<Cite s="2026-05-25-covert-operations-insight-chills" t="31:34" /><Cite s="2026-05-25-covert-operations-insight-chills" t="32:07" />

Per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou), O'Neill was not in fact an assistant: *"Eric O'Neill was actually there to spy on Hanssen to see if there were any slip-ups that would indicate that he was the mole."*<Cite s="2026-05-25-covert-operations-insight-chills" t="31:34" />

## After the arrest

The operation's psychological toll was significant. Per Kiriakou: *"The stress was so awful in this case that he quit the FBI after Hanssen was arrested."*<Cite s="2026-05-25-covert-operations-insight-chills" t="32:07" />

## *Breach*

The 2007 film *Breach* portrays the operation with O'Neill as protagonist. Per Kiriakou: *"Eric O'Neill is a hero in this movie."*<Cite s="2026-05-25-covert-operations-insight-chills" t="32:07" />
