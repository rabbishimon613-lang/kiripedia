**Cori Bush** is a former U.S. Representative from Missouri's 1st congressional district (St. Louis). She is cited by [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) — alongside [Jamaal Bowman](/wiki/jamaal-bowman) — as one of the two named cases of [AIPAC](/wiki/aipac) successfully primarying an incumbent House member after the member publicly characterized Israel's prosecution of the Gaza war as a *"wholesale massacre of Palestinian civilians and women and children."*<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:52:04" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:52:37" />

> Cori Bush, for example, in St. Louis — they threw her out on her ass. … Why? Because they said, you know, maybe the Israelis shouldn't be massacring all these women and children.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:52:37" />
