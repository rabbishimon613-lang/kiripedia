**Prince Muhammad bin Naif** is a Saudi royal and former head of Saudi intelligence. He survived an al-Qaeda assassination attempt in approximately 2009 in which the attacker had internally concealed an explosive device in his own body — *"the guy with the bomb up his ass."* [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) was asked in the 2025 Dalton Fischer interview whether the attack was directed by [Anwar al-Awlaki](/wiki/anwar-al-awlaki); Kiriakou stated that the rumor existed but he had never believed it to be correct, on the ground that al-Awlaki was *"more of a propagandist"* and was not operational.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:24:34" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:24:36" />
