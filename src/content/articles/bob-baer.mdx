**Robert "Bob" Baer** is a former CIA case officer. Per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou): *"Bob Baer is a pretty famous former CIA officer. He's written a bunch of books — one was made into the movie Syriana that starred George Clooney."*<Cite s="2024-11-13-julian-dorey-250" t="2:17:10" />

## The northern Iraq Saddam-assassination episode

Per Kiriakou, during the Clinton administration Baer *"took it on the chin"* after developing *"a network of sources in northern Iraq and was making plans to assassinate [Saddam Hussein](/wiki/saddam-hussein), which he believed to be in the national interest."* NSA intercepted one of his communications and forwarded it to [Sandy Berger](/wiki/sandy-berger), the National Security Adviser, who called the CIA: *"Your guy is going to do an extra-constitutional assassination. Either you pull him back, or he's under arrest for conspiracy."* The CIA pulled Baer out of Iraq.<Cite s="2024-11-13-julian-dorey-250" t="2:17:42" /><Cite s="2024-11-13-julian-dorey-250" t="2:18:12" />

Kiriakou: *"That would have changed history."*<Cite s="2024-11-13-julian-dorey-250" t="2:18:12" />
