**Imran Khan** is the former Prime Minister of [Pakistan](#), deposed and subsequently imprisoned. He continues, per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou)'s November 2025 account, to direct the affairs of his political party — the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf — from prison.<Cite s="2025-11-19-julian-dorey-vault-7" t="19:14" />

## The 2025 apology demand

In approximately early November 2025 Kiriakou gave an interview to an Indian news outlet in which he observed that *"in a conventional conflict India would beat Pakistan because it has five times the people."* The remark — which Kiriakou characterizes as *"I didn't say anything that I haven't said a thousand times"* — produced a quantity of Pakistani death threats sufficient that his attorney advised him to keep a low profile, and a formal letter from the president of Imran Khan's PTI party condemning the statement *"in the strongest possible terms"* and demanding an immediate apology to *"his excellency the former prime minister,"* to the party, and to the people of Pakistan.<Cite s="2025-11-19-julian-dorey-vault-7" t="15:08" /><Cite s="2025-11-19-julian-dorey-vault-7" t="15:38" /><Cite s="2025-11-19-julian-dorey-vault-7" t="16:40" />

Kiriakou's response — sent via email and self-reported in the same Julian Dorey appearance:

> In regards to your demand for an apology, I wiped my ass with your demands for an apology.<Cite s="2025-11-19-julian-dorey-vault-7" t="17:11" />

As a consequence Kiriakou has discontinued a previously scheduled speaking engagement at the Pakistani military academy. *"I'll never go back ever again. … But they don't treat me with respect anyway."*<Cite s="2025-11-19-julian-dorey-vault-7" t="17:11" />
