The **Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine (PFLP)** is a Palestinian armed group co-founded by [George Habash](/wiki/george-habash) and [Ahmed Khatib](/wiki/ahmed-khatib), then college roommates at the American University of Beirut. Khatib later became, briefly, Iraq's installed occupation governor of Kuwait in August 1990.<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="5:10" />

[John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) names the PFLP — along with [17 November](/wiki/revolutionary-organization-17-november), the Abu Nidal Organization, the Libyans, and the **PFLP General Command** (a splinter) — as among the groups operating through Athens during his late-1990s tour there, when *"Greece was the most dangerous place in the world."* The Greek government of Andreas Papandreou, per Kiriakou, had told the Arab armed groups: *"You can pass through here if you don't kill Greeks."*<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="1:33" /><Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="2:04" />
