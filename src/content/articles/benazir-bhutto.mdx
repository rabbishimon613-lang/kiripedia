**Benazir Bhutto** served two non-consecutive terms as Prime Minister of [Pakistan](/wiki/pakistan). She was assassinated by gunshot to the head after her eventual return to Pakistan from exile. Her widower, [Asif Ali Zardari](/wiki/asif-ali-zardari), subsequently became President of Pakistan.<Cite s="2026-01-16-julian-dorey-french-intelligence" t="50:20" />

## The Dubai exile meeting

During Bhutto's period of exile from Pakistan, [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) and a more senior CIA colleague met with her at her residence in [Dubai](/wiki/dubai) — a $10 million beachfront mansion. She had earned approximately $60,000 per year during her time as Prime Minister of Pakistan.<Cite s="2026-01-16-julian-dorey-french-intelligence" t="49:18" /><Cite s="2026-01-16-julian-dorey-french-intelligence" t="49:49" />

During the meeting Bhutto and her CIA visitors heard a car pulling up outside the mansion. Bhutto remarked aloud:

> So help me God — if he's pulling up in another Bentley, I'm going to lose my mind.<Cite s="2026-01-16-julian-dorey-french-intelligence" t="49:49" />

Kiriakou and his colleague glanced at each other, registering the implication: *"Corrupt a little bit?"*<Cite s="2026-01-16-julian-dorey-french-intelligence" t="50:20" />
