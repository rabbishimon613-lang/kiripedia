**Arrais Corporation** was a private intelligence firm, companion to [The Analysis Corporation](/wiki/analysis-corporation), originally created as an LLC pass-through for retired [Senior Intelligence Service](/wiki/cia) (SIS) personnel from the [Central Intelligence Agency](/wiki/cia)'s operations side — the parallel of The Analysis Corporation for the analytical side. After September 11, 2001, the firm became substantially commercial and was subsequently acquired by IBM for over $1 billion, making senior CIA operations retirees who held equity *"not just rich but … like, you know, megamillions lotto kind of rich."*<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="50:40" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="51:11" />
