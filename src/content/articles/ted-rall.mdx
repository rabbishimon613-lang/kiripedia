**Ted Rall** is an American editorial cartoonist and a two-time Pulitzer Prize finalist. He is the co-host with [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) of *Deprogram*, a daily news-commentary podcast launched in September 2025 — *"going every day starting next week at five o'clock on YouTube … far more of a news show, but it's light at the same time."*<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="4:11:03" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="4:11:34" />
