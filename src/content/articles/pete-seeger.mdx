**Pete Seeger** was an American folk singer and, per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou), *"besides my dad and my grandfather, Pete was the most important man in my life."*<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="51:56" />

## Relationship with Kiriakou

Kiriakou and Seeger exchanged Christmas cards for years. When Kiriakou was arrested in 2012, Seeger rallied to his defense. Per Kiriakou, Seeger once asked him: *"You and I have the greatest differences in our backgrounds — like, what is it about me that is attracting you?"* Kiriakou's answer:<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="52:26" />

> Pete, you stood up to them. In 1958, you stood up to them. All you had to say is, "On the advice of counsel, I decline to answer your question, Senator." And you didn't. You said, "Based on the First Amendment, I don't have to tell you with whom I associate." Everybody's pleading the Fifth — he pleaded the First, and they sent him to prison for it. It was overturned on appeal, but he wasn't afraid of them. And so I wanted to be like Pete.<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="52:58" />

The biography of Seeger, *How Can I Keep From Singing?*, is Kiriakou's favorite book.<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="51:56" />
