**Section 702** is the section of the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act under which the U.S. government conducts warrantless electronic surveillance. Per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) it is *"a section that directly takes our constitutional freedoms away from us in the name of national security."* It is the authority that NSA whistleblower [Tom Drake](/wiki/tom-drake) and Edward Snowden first made publicly known in the summer of 2013.<Cite s="2026-05-04-cleared-hot-446-cost-of-truth" t="40:02" /><Cite s="2026-05-04-cleared-hot-446-cost-of-truth" t="40:32" />

## Scale of misuse

Per Kiriakou, citing the Brennan Center for Justice: the FBI has made *"over 200,000"* illegal queries against the Section 702 database, including a recent two-year window in which there were approximately 78,000 misuse queries.<Cite s="2026-05-04-cleared-hot-446-cost-of-truth" t="40:32" /><Cite s="2026-05-04-cleared-hot-446-cost-of-truth" t="41:04" />

The Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Court is, in Kiriakou's framing, a rubber stamp: of ~34,000 FISA warrant applications, estimates range from 11 to 120 denied — *"hundredths of one percent."* Kiriakou's go-to example of the cost: *"Ask Carter Page how that worked out for him."*<Cite s="2026-05-04-cleared-hot-446-cost-of-truth" t="41:37" />

## The biennial renewal pattern

Section 702 comes up for reauthorization every two years. Each cycle, per Kiriakou, *"a couple more people will go over to the no side, but not in any numbers to actually mean something."*<Cite s="2026-05-04-cleared-hot-446-cost-of-truth" t="47:55" />

## The Trump reversal

Donald Trump campaigned against Section 702 in 2016. After taking office, per Kiriakou, Trump said *"he talked to three dozen generals and admirals and every single one of them said we have to have 702 to keep America safe. So I support the reauthorization of Section 702."* Kiriakou: *"Okay, that's exactly the opposite of what you ran on."*<Cite s="2026-05-04-cleared-hot-446-cost-of-truth" t="46:52" /><Cite s="2026-05-04-cleared-hot-446-cost-of-truth" t="47:24" />

## Why it persists

Kiriakou's diagnosis: there are no replacement systems being demanded, because there is no political will. *"How 702 keeps us safe when we have other electronic means to keep us just as safe without spying on American citizens unconstitutionally — nobody's ever explained."*<Cite s="2026-05-04-cleared-hot-446-cost-of-truth" t="47:24" />
