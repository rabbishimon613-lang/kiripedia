**Jonathan Pollard** is the U.S. Navy civilian intelligence analyst convicted in 1985 of spying for Israel. He is the canonical publicly proven case of Israeli espionage against the United States. [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) invokes Pollard's case in his account of his first appearance on Piers Morgan's program, in which he debated a former director of the [Mossad](/wiki/mossad) who denied any post-1985 Israeli espionage against the United States. Kiriakou's counter: *"Jonathan Pollard was caught in 1985, and you were spying on the United States in 1998, you know, or 2004."* The former Mossad director declined to comment.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:11:32" />
