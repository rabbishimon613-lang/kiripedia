**Robert MacLean** is a TSA whistleblower. Per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) he is *"an awesome guy … a serious, serious whistleblower."*<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:58:42" />

## Role in the Giuliani exposé

On the evening of the 2018 [Rudy Giuliani](/wiki/rudy-giuliani) pardon-extortion meeting, Kiriakou attended a Republican National Committee book launch in Washington at which MacLean was also present. Kiriakou described the meeting to MacLean. MacLean immediately called the FBI to report the attempted bribery; the FBI declined interest. MacLean then called the *New York Times*, which dispatched reporter Mike Schmidt to Kiriakou. The story ran on the Sunday front page.<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:58:42" /><Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="2:02:54" />
