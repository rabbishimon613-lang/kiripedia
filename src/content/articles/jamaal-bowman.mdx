**Jamaal Bowman** is a former U.S. Representative from New York. He is cited by [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) alongside [Cori Bush](/wiki/cori-bush) as the second documented case of [AIPAC](/wiki/aipac) successfully primarying an incumbent House member following the member's House floor remarks characterizing Israeli conduct in the Gaza war as the *"wholesale massacre of Palestinian civilians and women and children."*<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:52:04" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:52:37" />

> Jamaal Bowman in New York — out. Why? Because they said, you know, maybe the Israelis shouldn't be massacring all these women and children. Oh, how dare you. You must be anti-Semitic and they throw them out.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:52:37" />
