**Hassan Nasrallah** was the long-serving Secretary-General of [Hezbollah](/wiki/hezbollah), killed in an Israeli strike on a building complex in southern Lebanon. The strike achieved its strategic objective — the decapitation of Hezbollah's leadership — but at the cost of approximately 300 additional deaths from the same operation. [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) characterizes the operation, as he does the parallel scientist-killing strikes in the [Iran 12-Day War](/wiki/iran-12-day-war), as a war crime under the laws of armed conflict: *"They killed 300 people in southern Lebanon when they went after Hassan Nasrallah, the former head of Hezbollah, just blew up the entire complex. It decapitated Hezbollah. It was also a war crime."*<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:59:54" />
