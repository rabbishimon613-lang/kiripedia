**William J. "Bill" Casey** served as Director of Central Intelligence under President [Ronald Reagan](/wiki/ronald-reagan) during the 1980s. Within KiriPedia's source corpus he is referenced principally as the founder of the [CIA National Resources Division](/wiki/cia-national-resources-division), the agency's domestic intelligence-collection arm.<Cite s="2023-11-12-dalton-fischer-kiriakou-part-1" t="39:27" /><Cite s="2023-11-12-dalton-fischer-kiriakou-part-1" t="40:01" />

Casey is characterized as *"the notorious criminal that he was"* — but as having been *"actually right on"* about the National Resources Division concept. His insight: large numbers of patriotic American businesspeople routinely travel to "denied areas" — countries such as China, the Soviet Union, and Cuba where the CIA cannot maintain a clandestine presence — and would willingly debrief on what they had observed if asked overtly.<Cite s="2023-11-12-dalton-fischer-kiriakou-part-1" t="40:01" /><Cite s="2023-11-12-dalton-fischer-kiriakou-part-1" t="40:33" />
