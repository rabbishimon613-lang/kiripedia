**Mike Scheuer** was the founder and chief of "Alec Station," the CIA unit dedicated to tracking Osama bin Laden in the years before September 11, 2001. Per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou): *"Bill — Mike Scheuer — yeah. Two-junior. And Alec Station was saying from the very beginning, 'They're going to attack us, they're going to attack us, why is nobody paying attention, they're going to attack us.'"*<Cite s="2024-11-13-julian-dorey-250" t="58:50" /><Cite s="2024-11-13-julian-dorey-250" t="59:21" />

Per Kiriakou, Scheuer and FBI New York counterterrorism chief [John O'Neill](/wiki/john-oneill) were both *"100 percent correct"* about bin Laden — and both hated inside their own agencies for being so persistent about it.<Cite s="2024-11-13-julian-dorey-250" t="1:05:07" />
