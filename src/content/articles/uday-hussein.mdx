**Uday Hussein** was the eldest son of [Saddam Hussein](/wiki/saddam-hussein). In [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou)'s account he was the proximate cause of the January 1993 defection of Saddam's two sons-in-law — [Hussein Kamel](/wiki/hussein-kamel) and Saddam Kamel — to Jordan: both *"were afraid of Uday, and Uday saw both of them as potential challengers once Saddam had departed the scene,"* and *"it got to the point where … they decided to defect."*<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:36:47" />

Kiriakou's general assessment of Saddam's sons: *"His sons might have even been worse than he was."*<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="54:28" />
