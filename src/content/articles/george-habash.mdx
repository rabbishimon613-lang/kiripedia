**George Habash** was the co-founder of the [Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine](/wiki/pflp). Per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou), Habash and [Ahmed Khatib](/wiki/ahmed-khatib) — the medical doctor whom Iraq installed as occupation governor of Kuwait on August 2, 1990 — were college roommates at the American University of Beirut, where they together founded the PFLP.<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="4:40" /><Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="5:10" />
