**Mitchell and Jessen** are the two American psychologists who in late October 2001 pitched the so-called [enhanced interrogation techniques](/wiki/enhanced-interrogation) (EIT) program to [Central Intelligence Agency](/wiki/cia) Director [George Tenet](/wiki/george-tenet) at a cocktail party. The CIA signed their contract in January 2002. The first detainee on whom the program was applied was [Abu Zubaydah](/wiki/abu-zubaydah), captured in [Pakistan](/wiki/pakistan) in March 2002.<Cite s="2026-01-16-julian-dorey-french-intelligence" t="55:34" /><Cite s="2026-01-16-julian-dorey-french-intelligence" t="56:05" />
