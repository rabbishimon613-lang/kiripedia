**John Walker Lindh** is the American citizen captured while fighting alongside the Taliban during the early phase of the U.S. campaign in Afghanistan. He was present at the [Qala-i-Jangi fortress](/wiki/qala-i-jangi-uprising) in northern Afghanistan at the start of the late-November 2001 prisoner uprising in which CIA officer [Mike Spann](/wiki/mike-spann) was killed.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:37:09" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:37:39" />
