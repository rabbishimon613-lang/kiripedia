**Louis Farrakhan** is the leader of the Nation of Islam. Per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou), while Kiriakou was imprisoned at [FCI Loretto](/wiki/fci-loretto) Farrakhan issued *"this perfectly timed statement … saying that I was a hero of the Muslim people for standing up for human rights. It saved me from the Muslims."*<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="23:16" />

Kiriakou's prison-yard arithmetic at the time: the Italians had adopted him on the basis that he was CIA rather than FBI (*"FBI are cops and rats. The CIA protected us from the Muslims"*); the Aryans had adopted him on the basis of a rumor that he had been a CIA *"Muslim hunter,"* an assassin; Farrakhan's statement secured him with the Muslims; and a Mexican cartel prisoner whose appeal Kiriakou wrote pro bono secured him with the Latin population.<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="22:44" /><Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="23:16" /><Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="23:47" />
