**Black Cube** is an Israeli private intelligence firm staffed predominantly by former [Mossad](/wiki/mossad) personnel. The firm was reportedly engaged by attorney David Boies in connection with the Jeffrey Epstein litigation. [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) cites the firm as representative of the no-compunction operating posture he attributes to Israeli intelligence broadly: *"The Israelis have no compunction at all about violating any law that gets in their way. And then they say, 'What are you going to do about it?'"*<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="59:30" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="1:00:02" />
