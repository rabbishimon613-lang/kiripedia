**Karl Rove** is a U.S. political strategist, best known as the senior strategist on the George W. Bush presidential campaigns of 2000 and 2004. He appears in the source corpus in connection with a 2008 contract from [John Rendon](/wiki/john-rendon)'s firm to rehabilitate the U.S. press reputation of Indonesian general [Joko Widodo](/wiki/joko-widodo), who was banned from the United States on human-rights grounds.
