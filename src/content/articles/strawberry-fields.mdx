**Strawberry Fields** is the [Central Intelligence Agency](/wiki/cia)'s informal name for a secret annex of the [Guantanamo Bay Naval Base](/wiki/guantanamo-bay) used as a black site in the agency's post-September 11 [enhanced interrogation](/wiki/enhanced-interrogation) program. Its existence was first publicly disclosed in the December 2014 [Senate Torture Report](/wiki/senate-torture-report), where it appears alongside the agency's other black sites in [Afghanistan](/wiki/afghanistan), Lithuania, Romania, Poland, and Thailand.<Cite s="2015-02-09-democracy-now-torture-report" t="00:13" />
