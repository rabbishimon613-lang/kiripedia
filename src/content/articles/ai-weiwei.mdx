**Ai Weiwei** is the Chinese contemporary artist and pro-democracy activist who designed the Beijing Olympic Stadium and was subsequently imprisoned in China for his activism. Per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou), Ai produced a Lego portrait series of 178 political prisoners while Kiriakou was himself in federal prison. Kiriakou's panel sits in the series next to one of Martin Luther King Jr.<Cite s="2026-05-25-covert-operations-insight-chills" t="00:36" /><Cite s="2026-05-25-covert-operations-insight-chills" t="01:09" />

The work now lives in the permanent collection of the Smithsonian Institution.<Cite s="2026-05-25-covert-operations-insight-chills" t="01:09" />
