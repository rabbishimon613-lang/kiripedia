**Mike Baker** is a former [Central Intelligence Agency](/wiki/cia) case officer. He was [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou)'s direct predecessor at the Athens station during the period in which the CIA was working the [Revolutionary Organization 17 November](/wiki/revolutionary-organization-17-november) problem. *"Mike's a great guy. He was a good officer. He was uh — he doesn't really talk about his work a lot. Maybe it's cuz a lot of years have passed, but he was the real deal."*<Cite s="2025-10-10-joe-rogan-experience-2392" t="00:01" />

Per Kiriakou, much of the preliminary intelligence groundwork on 17 November had been completed by Baker before Kiriakou inherited the position.<Cite s="2025-10-10-joe-rogan-experience-2392" t="00:01" />
