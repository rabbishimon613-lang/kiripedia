**Noelle Dunphy** was an aide to [Rudy Giuliani](/wiki/rudy-giuliani) at the time of his 2018 $2 million pardon-extortion attempt against [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou). Per Kiriakou, on the evening of the meeting — back in New York — Giuliani told Dunphy that he had *"tried to get $2 million out of this guy for a pardon today."* Dunphy retained the information until Giuliani's subsequent sexual harassment of her, at which point she contacted the *New York Times* and corroborated Kiriakou's account in full.<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="2:08:13" /><Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="2:08:45" />
