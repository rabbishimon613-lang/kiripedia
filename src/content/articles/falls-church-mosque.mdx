The **Falls Church mosque** is a large mosque located on Route 7 in Falls Church, Virginia. In 1993, the imam was [Anwar al-Awlaki](/wiki/anwar-al-awlaki). [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) visited the mosque that year as part of a CIA Arabic-language-training field trip — a day-long itinerary of Arab-American businesses in Falls Church under a no-English-permitted rule — that ended at the mosque for a tour and Q&A with the imam.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:25:05" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:25:37" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:26:08" />

Eight years later, the September 11 hijackers prayed at the same mosque the night before the attacks.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="3:26:08" />
