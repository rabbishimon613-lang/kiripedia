**Hussein Kamel al-Majid** was [Saddam Hussein](/wiki/saddam-hussein)'s son-in-law and Iraq's Minister of Industry and Military Industrialization. He and his brother Saddam Kamel — head of the Special Republican Guard, married to Saddam's other daughter — defected to Jordan in January 1993 out of fear of [Uday Hussein](/wiki/uday-hussein), whom both saw as a fatal threat once Saddam left the scene.<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:35:13" /><Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:36:47" /><Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:37:19" />

## CIA interview in Amman

King Hussein of Jordan invited a CIA delegation to interview the brothers. [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) was among them. *"One of the things that struck me immediately was they were wearing sidearms. … You're a guest of the king, you're asking for refuge in a royal palace, and you're carrying a sidearm — like, who do you think you are?"*<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:37:19" /><Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:37:49" />

Hussein Kamel opened with a demand: weapons, tanks, air cover, secure communications — i.e., U.S. backing for an overthrow of Saddam. Kiriakou's boss: *"Whoa whoa whoa whoa, we're not here to help you overthrow Saddam Hussein, we would never do that. We're here for you to tell us where the WMD is."* Kamel refused: *"I'm not telling you where the WMD is — you're going to help me overthrow Saddam Hussein."* He did not deny possessing it. Per Kiriakou: *"I'm confident they had WMD in 1993"* — definitely chemical weapons, *"because they had used chemical weapons against the Kurds and the Iranians,"* and possibly biological.<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:37:49" /><Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:38:20" /><Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:38:50" />

## The CIA leak that burned him

When the U.S. would not commit to overthrowing Saddam, the agency leaked a false story to the Arab press claiming that a secret CIA delegation had met the Kamel brothers in Amman and that the brothers had told the Americans everything. Per Kiriakou, Saddam saw the article and destroyed the chemical-weapons stockpile. *"That was successful — that's a good leak. The only remnants of chemical weapons we were [finding later] …"*<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:39:54" />
