**Bernard "Bernie" Kerik** is a former New York Police Department Commissioner. In [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou)'s account he is the person who made the 2018 introduction between Kiriakou and [Rudy Giuliani](/wiki/rudy-giuliani) that culminated in Giuliani's $2 million pardon-extortion attempt at the Trump International Hotel in Washington, D.C.<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:55:32" />
