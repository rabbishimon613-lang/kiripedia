**Jeffrey Epstein** was a convicted sex offender who died in U.S. federal custody. [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou)'s position on him, on the Carlos Watson program:<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="48:49" />

## "An Israeli access agent"

Asked whether Epstein worked for the CIA: *"No way. … To spy on whom? The CIA's job is to collect foreign intelligence. What foreign intelligence did he have? He's spying on Bill Clinton and Bill Gates and Prince Andrew? That's the Israelis."*<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="48:49" /><Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="49:21" />

## Suicide

Kiriakou believes Epstein died by suicide. His reasoning, drawn from his own time in the Bureau of Prisons: *"In the Bureau of Prisons everybody has his head up his ass. The cameras never work. The guards are always sound asleep. This happens all the time. These suicides — they happen literally every day in American prisons. Every day. When the only qualifications to be a prison guard are you have to be working on a GED or better and no felony convictions, you get what you pay for."*<Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="49:21" /><Cite s="2026-04-27-carlos-watson-part-everyone-missed" t="49:51" />
