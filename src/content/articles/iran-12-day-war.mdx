The **Iran 12-Day War** was a 2025 Israeli air campaign against Iran with closing U.S. participation. It is invoked by [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou) as the operationally and morally categorical departure of Israeli targeting doctrine from U.S. and CIA doctrine, in particular with regard to acceptable-civilian-casualty calculus.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:57:18" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:58:19" />

## Scientist-killing campaign

The Israeli effort killed at least fourteen Iranian nuclear scientists by destroying the apartment buildings in which they resided. For each scientist, the campaign was preceded by a coordinated telephone call from an Iranian-Jewish [Mossad](/wiki/mossad) or Shin Bet officer — operating in Farsi — to the scientist's personal cell phone, offering defection in exchange for survival; a parallel call was placed to the scientist's wife. *"Listen, you're going to die tomorrow, right? We're going to kill you tomorrow. You have no hope. You're going to die. Just accept it. Or you can defect to us right now."* When the scientists declined, the apartment buildings were destroyed, killing the scientists' families in addition to the scientists themselves.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:58:19" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:58:50" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:59:21" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:59:54" />

The operation has continued past the immediate killing: per Kiriakou, Israeli intelligence is now mapping the next generation of Iranian nuclear scientists.<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:59:54" />

## Contrast with CIA doctrine

Kiriakou contrasts the campaign with CIA targeted-killing practice, which proceeds — at least in standing orders — under a "fewest civilian casualties as humanly possible" rule, normally via either drone strike on the target alone or in-person close-in actions. *"For there to be a half-dozen people killed in a drone attack — that's unusual."* The Israeli policy, by contrast: *"They will take out the entire city block if there's one target in one of those apartment buildings that they want to get."*<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:57:18" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:57:49" />

## U.S. closing role

The campaign concluded with U.S. participation: a single strike using *"the biggest bomb that humankind has ever seen"* — the basis on which President Donald Trump sought to broker a return to negotiations on the Iranian nuclear program. Kiriakou's assessment of the diplomatic logic is unfavorable: *"What's the incentive for them to do what you want a second time? Why would they trust you?"*<Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:47:17" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:47:49" /><Cite s="2025-08-31-dalton-fischer-mossad-blackwater" t="2:48:20" />
