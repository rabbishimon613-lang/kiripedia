**Morton H. Halperin** is a former Assistant Secretary of State, Assistant Secretary of Defense, and senior director of the National Security Council. Per [John Kiriakou](/wiki/john-kiriakou), Halperin authored the statute under which Kiriakou was convicted — the Intelligence Identities Protection Act.<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:54:00" />

After Kiriakou's conviction, Halperin wrote to President Obama stating that *"this is not why he wrote the law and that I should be"* pardoned. The pardon was not granted under the Obama administration.<Cite s="2024-11-07-julian-dorey-249" t="1:54:00" />
